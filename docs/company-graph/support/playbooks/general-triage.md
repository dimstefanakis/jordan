# General Triage

## Vague Or Incomplete Request

### Classification

`ask-more-info`

### Customer-Safe Guidance

Ask for the minimum missing details needed to route the issue safely.

### Human/Admin Action

None by default.

### Notes

Do not invent missing context.

## Confirmed Product Bug

### Classification

`human-check-required`

### Customer-Safe Guidance

Acknowledge the reported behavior and avoid promising a fix timeline unless one is already documented.

### Human/Admin Action

Escalate to the appropriate owner when investigation or a fix is required.

### Notes

If the bug reveals a durable knowledge gap, update the company graph after the incident is understood.
