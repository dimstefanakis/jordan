# Identity And Ownership

## Definition

Define how the system recognizes a user, customer, workspace, or account owner.

## How It Works

Document the durable identifiers, matching rules, and state transitions that matter for reasoning.

## Where It Shows Up

- onboarding
- account recovery
- ownership disputes
- audit trails

## Disclosure Boundary

- Safe to disclose: high-level rules users need to understand.
- Do not disclose: private heuristics, abuse tooling, or sensitive matching logic.
